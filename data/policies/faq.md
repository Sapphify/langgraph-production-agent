# Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Orders

**Q: How do I create an account?**
A: Click "Sign Up" on our website and enter your email address and a password. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

**Q: How do I track my order?**
A: Log in to your account and go to "My Orders", or use the tracking number from your shipping confirmation email.

**Q: Can I change or cancel my order?**
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order enters processing and cannot be changed. Contact us immediately if you need to make changes.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

## Returns & Refunds

**Q: How long do refunds take?**
A: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.

**Q: Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?**
A: Yes! Contact our customer service team to arrange an exchange. The new item will ship once we receive the original.

**Q: What if I received a damaged or wrong item?**
A: Contact us immediately with photos of the issue. We will ship the correct item or a replacement at no additional cost.

## Technical Support

**Q: My product isn't working. What should I do?**
A: Try restarting the device and checking our troubleshooting guide on the product page. If the issue persists, contact us for warranty support.

**Q: Do you offer setup assistance?**
A: Yes, we provide free setup guides for all products. For hands-on help, our technical support team is available via chat or phone.
