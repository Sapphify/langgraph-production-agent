# Shipping Policy

## Shipping Options
- **Standard Shipping**: 5-7 business days. Free on orders over $50, otherwise $5.99.
- **Express Shipping**: 2-3 business days. $12.99 flat rate.
- **Overnight Shipping**: Next business day delivery. $24.99 flat rate.

## Order Processing
- Orders placed before 2:00 PM EST are processed the same business day.
- Orders placed after 2:00 PM EST or on weekends are processed the next business day.

## Tracking
- A tracking number is emailed once your order ships.
- Track your order at any time using the tracking number on our website or the carrier's site.

## International Shipping
- We currently ship to the US and Canada only.
- International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the customer's responsibility.

## Delivery Issues
- If your package is marked as delivered but not received, please wait 48 hours as carriers sometimes mark early.
- After 48 hours, contact us and we will open an investigation with the carrier.
- Lost or stolen packages will be replaced or refunded at our discretion.
