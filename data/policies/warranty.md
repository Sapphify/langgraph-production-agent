# Warranty Policy

## Standard Warranty
- All products come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty from the date of purchase.
- The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.

## What Is Covered
- Manufacturing defects
- Faulty components
- Hardware malfunctions not caused by misuse

## What Is NOT Covered
- Physical damage (drops, spills, cracks)
- Normal wear and tear
- Damage from unauthorized modifications or repairs
- Water damage (unless the product is rated as waterproof)

## How to Claim Warranty
1. Contact our customer service team with your order ID and a description of the issue.
2. We may ask for photos or a video demonstrating the defect.
3. If approved, we will either repair, replace, or refund the item.
4. Warranty replacements are shipped free of charge.

## Extended Warranty
- An extended warranty (additional 1 year) can be purchased within 30 days of the original order for 15% of the product price.
- Extended warranty claims follow the same process as standard warranty claims.
