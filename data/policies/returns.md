# Return Policy

## Standard Returns
- Items may be returned within 30 days of delivery for a full refund.
- Products must be in original packaging and unused condition.
- Return shipping is free for defective items. For other returns, a $5.99 return shipping fee applies.

## Exceptions
- Electronics must be returned within 15 days of delivery.
- Personalized or custom items cannot be returned unless defective.
- Gift cards are non-refundable.

## Refund Processing
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item.
- Original payment method will be credited. If the original method is unavailable, store credit will be issued.
- Partial refunds may be issued for items returned in opened or used condition at our discretion.

## How to Start a Return
1. Contact our customer service team with your order ID.
2. We will provide a return authorization number and shipping label.
3. Pack the item securely and ship it back using the provided label.
4. You will receive an email confirmation once the refund is processed.
